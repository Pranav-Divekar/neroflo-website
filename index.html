What we build

AI solutions, not off-the-shelf software

Every project is different. Here are the kinds of problems we typically solve.

Automation
Cutting out manual, repetitive work

If your team is spending hours on tasks that follow a pattern — reading documents, routing requests, extracting data, writing first drafts — we can usually automate most of it.

  • Document processing and data extraction
  • Email and support ticket classification
  • Report generation from structured data
  • Internal approval and routing workflows
Internal tools
Making your data actually usable

Most companies have useful data locked in PDFs, spreadsheets, or siloed systems. We build tools that let your team ask questions and get answers without digging through files.

  • Internal knowledge bases your team can query
  • Search across contracts, policies, past work
  • Onboarding tools built on existing documentation
  • Custom dashboards with AI-generated summaries
Customer-facing
AI that your customers interact with

We build AI features that sit inside your existing product or website — things like smart assistants, recommendation systems, or automated responses that actually sound like your brand.

  • Support bots trained on your actual documentation
  • Product recommendation or configurator flows
  • Personalised content and communications
  • Conversational interfaces for complex products
Data and analysis
Turning raw data into decisions

AI is genuinely good at finding patterns in large amounts of unstructured text — feedback, reviews, call transcripts, emails. We build pipelines that surface what matters.

  • Customer feedback analysis at scale
  • Competitor monitoring and summarisation
  • Call transcript analysis and tagging
  • Anomaly detection with plain-language alerts
How we work

From first conversation to working software

We keep the process simple. No long discovery phases, no consultancy overhead.

01
Discovery call

We talk through your problem, your existing setup, and your team. This usually takes an hour. We'll tell you honestly if we think AI is the right tool.

02
Scoping and proposal

We write up what we'd build, how long it would take, and what it would cost. No ambiguity. You decide if you want to move forward.

03
Build and iterate

We build in short cycles and check in regularly. You see progress early and give feedback before anything is finalised.

04
Handover and support

We hand over working software your team can actually use. We document everything, train your people, and stay available for questions.

Why work with us

We're a small team. That's the point.

We're not a large agency with account managers in the middle. The people you talk to are the people who build. That means faster feedback loops, less overhead, and a team that's genuinely invested in whether the solution works.

We're early, which means we're selective about the projects we take on. We work with companies where we think we can do our best work and where the problem is real.

We won't pitch you something you don't need. If we don't think AI is the right answer for your problem, we'll say so in the first call.

You own everything
